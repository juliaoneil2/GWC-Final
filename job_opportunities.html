Job Opportunities

General Job Information

It is important to secure a job as quickly as possible, even if it is not the job of your dreams. You can work your way up to that point!

Many companies are looking to hire immigrants, so here is your chance! The industries most populated with immigrants are as follows:

  • Customer service
    • This can be especially helpful if you are bilingual--companies are eager to have somebody to translate to reach a better audience!
  • Manual labor jobs
    • Easy to get a job
    • ex) Construction workers, stone masons, maintenance technician, painter
  • Agricultural Working
    • Ranch/farm managers
    • Groundskeeping
    • Landscaping
    • Greenhouse management
  • Information Technology
  • Manufacturing
    • Factory working
  • Retail
    • Working in stores, selling items to customers

Resume Building

A resume is a written compilation of your education, work experience, credentials, and accomplishments. Most professional positions require applicants to submit a resume and cover letter as part of the application process.

Tips for Building a Resume

  • Keep it to one page!
  • Use bullets to organize your information, just like the informaton on this page
  • Use a professional email address
  • Use action verbs to explain your jobs and successes
  • Proofread and have somebody else read it for you!
